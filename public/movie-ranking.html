My top 3 movies of all time


The Pursuit of happiness

A very good drama movie that shows you should never give up

Gifted

Gifted is a drama film with elements of family and coming-of-age. It mixes heartfelt emotional storytelling with themes of parenting, education, and what it means to live a meaningful life.

Action-packed, emotional stakes, strong performances, suspenseful, family-driven, intense storytelling.
